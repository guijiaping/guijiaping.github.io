About Me

I started working as a researcher at NEC Labs America in 2018. I obtained Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Southern California in 2019. My Ph.D. supervisor was Prof. William G.J. Halfond. Before that, I received both my M.E. and B.S.E. degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2012 and 2010, respectively. I also received a dual-M.S. degree from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2012.

My research interests are computer security (current project), and software engineering (earlier projects in school).
