About Me

I am currently a tenure-track assistant professor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Southern California in 2019. My research interests are in network and system security, AI in security, and software engineering, with a focus on designing a next generation of security systems using AI technology.

I am always looking for highly self-motivated PhD/master/undergraduate students (including visiting students) to work with me. Please drop me an email with your CV if you are interested.
